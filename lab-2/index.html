Colors based on CIL color paint swatch cards

To make the colours on screen match the physical paint chips as closely as possible, I disabled any blue-light features (such as Night Light), kept my monitor brightness consistent, and compared the digital swatches directly against the paint card under neutral indoor lighting. I also avoided comparing in areas with warm/yellow lighting because it changed how the greens and browns looked. After adjusting, I found that display settings and room lighting make a noticeable difference in how close the screen colours appear compared to the printed paint chip colours.
